About Us

VMSNA is a volunteer-led, non-profit community organization dedicated to preserving heritage and building connections among TN Vokkaligas in North America.

Purpose

To create a welcoming home for community, culture, and service: a place where families connect, youth grow into confident leaders, and seniors remain engaged and celebrated.

About TN Vokkaligas

Vokkaligas (also known as Gowdas) are a prominent agrarian community with deep roots across southern India. VMSNA focuses on the Tamil Nadu Vokkaliga diaspora, celebrating language, customs, festivals, and shared values of hard work, learning, and service.
